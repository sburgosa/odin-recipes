Quesadillas

A traditional mexican dish, delicious quesadillas

Ingredients

  • 2 medium size tortillas
  • 100 gr of Mozarella cheese
  • extra ingrediente that you preffer
  • some suggestions are: pulled pork
  • Chicken, or bacon.

Process

  1. Heat the tortillas in an iron pan
  2. Turn it when you see a small burning
  3. Put the cheese half the way, later we will fold it in half.
  4. Add any extra ingredient that you want.
  5. Fold it in half, if you want, you can heat it a little more.
  6. Serve on a plate and sprinkle some cilantro

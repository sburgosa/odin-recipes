Neapolitan Pasta

Neapolitan pasta dish with penne

Ingredients

  • 500 gr very red tomatoes
  • 230 gr of pasta
  • Basil, hopefully fresh
  • Boiling water

Process

  1. Chop the tomatoes and cook them softly on a pan
  2. Put the pasta to cook in the boiling pot of water
  3. Add the basil to the tomatoes and stirr firmly
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. When the pasta is al dente, mix it with the tomatoes
  6. Serve on a plate

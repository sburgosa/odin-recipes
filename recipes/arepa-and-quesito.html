Arepa and Quesito

Arepa con quesito perfect for breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 medium size arepas
  • 6 slices of quesito
  • Some butter

Process

  1. Heat the arepas in an iron tray
  2. Turn it gradually so it does not burn only one side
  3. Serve on a plate and add 3 slices of quesito.
  4. If you want, you can cut the quesito into pieces or smash it.
